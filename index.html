South African Wildlife Conservation

Witness South Africa's amazing Wildlife

South Africa is home to diverse ecosystems and amazing wildlife. From the Big Five to countless bird species, the country offers some of the most remarkable wildlife viewing opportunities on the planet.

Amazing Facts About South African Wildlife:

  • South Africa is home to more than 300 mammal species
  • The country contains nearly 10% of all known bird species on Earth
  • Kruger National Park is one of Africa's largest game reserves
  • South Africa has implemented innovative conservation strategies to protect endangered species
